The Composition of Power: Insights into MEP Demographics and Roles

An In-depth Analysis of the Composition and Dynamics of the European Parliament (2019-2024)

By Dimitris Elafropoulos

The European Parliament (EP) plays a crucial role in the legislative process of the European Union, representing the interests of over 447 million citizens. This analysis provides insights into the composition and dynamics of the EP for the 2019-2024 term, focusing on gender representation, political distribution, roles within the parliament, and committee memberships.
